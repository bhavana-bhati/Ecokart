Nature's fastest growing plant

It's a Grass

Bamboo is not a tree - it's actually a giant type of grass that grows incredibly fast.

Super Fast

Some bamboo can grow 35 inches in one day, making it the world's fastest growing plant.

Strong & Flexible

Bamboo is both very strong and very flexible - it bends in wind but rarely breaks.

Hollow Stems

The stems are hollow with rings called nodes, making them lightweight but very strong.

Many Types

There are over 1,400 species of bamboo around the world.

Warm Climates

Bamboo grows best in tropical and warm climates with plenty of rain and sun.
