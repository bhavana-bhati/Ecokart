How bamboo is collected and made into useful products

Collecting Bamboo

Bamboo is collected from sustainable forests. Workers carefully cut the bamboo stalks at the right age to ensure they are strong and flexible.

Processing Bamboo

After collection, the bamboo is cleaned and treated to remove any impurities. It is then cut into smaller pieces for easier handling.

Shaping Products

The processed bamboo is shaped into various products. This includes bending, cutting, and assembling to create items like kitchenware and furniture.

Bamboo Kitchenware

Bamboo is made into kitchen items such as utensils, bowls, and cutting boards. These products are durable, lightweight, and eco-friendly.

Bamboo Furniture

Bamboo is used to create stylish furniture like chairs, tables, and shelves. It is strong and can be designed in many different styles.

Bamboo Accessories

Bamboo is crafted into various accessories, including vases, coasters, and decorative items. These products add a natural touch to any home.
